2-Day Easy Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 2 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Contact our customer service within 48 hours of receiving your order
  • Provide your order number and reason for return
  • We'll WhatsApp you a return authorization and instructions

Conditions for Returns

  • Item must be unused, unworn, and in original condition with tags attached
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase required

Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Delivery Within 4 Days

We guarantee delivery of your order within 4 business days for most locations in our service area.

Delivery Areas

Our 4-day delivery guarantee applies to all major cities and towns within our service network. Some remote areas may experience slightly longer delivery times.

Tracking Your Order

  • You will receive a tracking number via email/SMS once your order ships
  • Track your package in real-time through our website or mobile app
  • Receive delivery notifications and estimated time of arrival

Delivery Exceptions

Delivery times may be affected by circumstances beyond our control including weather conditions, customs delays for international orders, or incorrect address information provided.

Return & Exchange Info
